🧼 Link Cleaner

Paste a URL to remove tracking parameters like utm_source, si, or fbclid.

✅ Works best with links from:
  • YouTube (preserves v, t, list)
  • News articles (e.g., CNN, NYTimes, etc.)
  • Online stores (Amazon, etc. to remove affiliate tags)
❌ Doesn't support shortened URLs like bit.ly unless expanded.
🔒 Bank, government, or auth-related links are blocked for safety.
All processing is local—nothing is sent anywhere.
Version: v4.1
